## Core Public Facts
- Mallorca has 24 golf courses in total.
- 21 are open to green-fee visitors.
- 3 are private or restricted.
- Santa Ponsa II and Santa Ponsa III are shareholder-access only.
- La Reserva Rotana is for hotel and restaurant guests only and is currently closed for refurbishment until June 2026.
- Public 18-hole green fees now run from around `€55` at the value end to around `€252` at peak at Son Muntaner.
- Older broad one-line green-fee claims are out of date.
- Andy has now played Son Muntaner, so Son Muntaner can now be treated as genuine first-hand experience in public copy.

## The Real Price Pattern
Peak season:
- roughly mid-March to early June
- and mid-September to mid-November

This is when:
- courses are usually at their most expensive
- conditions are often at their best
- demand is strongest

Value windows:
- June to August
- December to February

Editorial implication:
- do not say October to April is the cheaper part of the year
- do not imply spring and autumn are budget windows
- if budget matters, point readers toward summer mornings / twilight or winter

Editorial rule:
- where pricing is dynamic, use typical ranges and explain that earlier booking usually secures the better rate
- avoid presenting one exact figure as if it were permanently published

## Twilight
Twilight usually starts around `15:00-16:00`, earlier in winter and later in summer.

Use publicly:
- twilight is one of the easiest ways to access Mallorca's better courses for materially less money
- many premium courses discount heavily later in the day

Useful examples:
- Son Muntaner: around `€252` peak down to around `€127` twilight
- Son Vida: around `€191` peak down to around `€93` twilight
- Son Antem East: around `€133-140` peak down to around `€90` twilight
- T Golf Calvia: around `€210` peak down to around `€169` twilight

## Buggies
- A buggy is not included at most Mallorca courses.
- Typical buggy cost is around `€40-50` on top of the green fee.
- Some Arabella bookings, including Son Muntaner, include a shared buggy from March through November.
- Golf Andratx offers a single-rider buggy at around `€30`.

Walking guidance:
- Hilly, buggy strongly recommended: Andratx, Son Termens back nine, Vall d'Or
- Hilly, buggy recommended: Bendinat, Alcanada, Pollensa, Canyamel front nine
- Undulating but walkable: Maioris, Santa Ponsa, Son Gual, Son Quint, Son Muntaner, Son Vida, T Golf Calvia, T Golf Palma, Pula
- Flat / easily walkable: Son Antem East, Son Antem West, Son Servera

## Club Hire
- Club hire is available at every course.
- Entry-level sets run from around `€25` at Pollensa to around `€36` at Alcanada.
- Some clubs offer standard vs premium options.
- Son Gual, Andratx, and Son Antem typically show standard Callaway around `€35-40`.
- Premium Titleist is usually around `€40-45`.
- Maioris hires Wilson Staff D300 clubs at around `€33`.

## Range Balls
- Included at some courses, not all.
- Son Muntaner includes range balls before the round.
- T Golf Calvia includes range balls with the green fee.
- Elsewhere, expect around `€3-5` per basket.

## Golf Licence / Insurance
Some courses add a mandatory daily golf licence / insurance charge of around `€3` per person per round.

Known public examples:
- Son Muntaner
- Alcanada
- Golf Andratx

Editorial rule:
- keep licence cost separate from the green fee
- do not bury it inside the main green-fee number if you can avoid it

## Reliability Levels
### Confirmed
Safe to use publicly with normal confidence.

Typical cases:
- official live pricing page confirmed
- official rate page or tariff visible
- latest official double-check agrees with internal notes

Confirmed courses (as of March-April 2026 live check):
- Son Gual, Alcanada, Real Golf Bendinat, Golf Pollensa, Golf Son Termes, Vall d'Or, Canyamel, Capdepera, Golf Son Servera, Son Antem memberships

### Partial / use with caution
Usable with caveat or range-based phrasing only.

Typical cases:
- live page still shows prior-year labelling
- dynamic pricing floors rather than one fixed tariff
- offer-led pricing rather than a clean annual card

Examples: Santa Ponsa I, Real Golf Bendinat (page dated 2025), Canyamel (page dated 2025), Golf Maioris

### Verify before publishing
Do not state as a hard public fact.

Typical cases:
- dynamic pricing only
- third-party-only figure
- unclear validity date
- membership pricing with no clean current public page

Examples: Arabella courses (Son Muntaner, Son Vida, Son Quint), T Golf Palma, T Golf Calvia, Pula, Son Antem East/West green fees, Golf de Andratx

## Editorial Rules
- Prefer ranges over one exact number if the course uses dynamic pricing.
- Make seasonality explicit.
- Separate green fee, buggy, club hire, range, and licence costs where relevant.
- Do not say October to April is the cheap season.
- Do not collapse all Mallorca green fees into one old generic range.
- If the price depends on live booking-engine demand, say so plainly.
