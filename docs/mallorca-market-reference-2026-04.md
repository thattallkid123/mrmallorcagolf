## Core facts
- Mallorca has 24 golf courses in total.
- 21 are open to green-fee visitors.
- Santa Ponsa II and III are shareholder-only.
- Reserva Rotana is restricted to hotel / restaurant guests and is closed for refurbishment until June 2026.
- Andy has now played Son Muntaner, so Son Muntaner is safe to treat as first-hand experience.

## Pricing reality
- Older generic one-line price ranges are out of date.
- Public 18-hole pricing now runs from roughly `EUR 55` to `EUR 252`.
- Peak pricing is usually mid-March to early June and mid-September to mid-November.
- Better value is usually found in June to August and December to February.
- Many Mallorca courses now use dynamic pricing; for comparisons and sorting, anchor on the peak public 18-hole price rather than winter or maintenance lows.

## Practical planning points
- Twilight is often the easiest way to reduce cost on premium courses.
- A buggy is usually extra and often costs around `?40-50`.
- Some courses add a daily golf licence / insurance fee of around `?3`.
- Club hire is available everywhere and usually runs roughly `?25-45` depending on course and set quality.

## Editorial rules
- Do not say October to April is the cheap season.
- Do not present dynamic-pricing courses as if they have one permanent fixed tariff.
- Keep Andy's service pricing separate from course market pricing.
- If a figure is dynamic, use a range or say pricing is dynamic.
